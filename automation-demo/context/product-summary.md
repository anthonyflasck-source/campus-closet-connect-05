# Product Summary

## What It Does
Campus Closet is a marketplace for dresses where college students can buy or rent dresses for events. Users can browse listings posted by other students, find dresses they like, and connect directly with the poster to arrange the transaction.

## Who It Is For
The product is mainly designed for college women managing frequent campus events and social pressure around what to wear.

## Current Status
- Live URL: https://campuscloset10.lovable.app/
- Stage: Live prototype available for real use and feedback.

## The One-Liner
A college-focused dress marketplace where students can buy, rent, and message each other to coordinate dress transactions for campus events.
